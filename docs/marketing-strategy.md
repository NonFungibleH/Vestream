# Vestream Marketing Strategy

**Goal:** Awareness / brand + website traffic + app downloads
**Budget posture:** Organic-first (small optional paid layer)
**Capacity:** Daily, part-time
**Date:** 2026-06-09

---

## The core thesis: market with data nobody else has

Most crypto products market by shouting ("we're live, download us"). Vestream
doesn't need to — it sits on **unique data**: every wallet receiving vested
tokens and every upcoming unlock across all protocols and chains. That data is
both the **content** (genuinely useful unlock info people want) and a **product
demo** at the same time.

Every post should do double duty: be useful on its own AND show what Vestream
does. The content *is* the product.

Two ways to weaponize the data:
1. **As content** → the "Unlock Radar" engine (drives awareness + traffic)
2. **As targeting** → reach actual token recipients during their unlock moment
   (drives downloads)

---

## Positioning

**One-liner:** *Vestream is the radar for token unlocks — find every token
vesting to your wallet, get alerted before it unlocks, and export it for taxes.*

**The wedge:** "What's vesting to MY wallet?" Nobody else answers that for
retail. Unlock-calendar sites (Token Unlocks, etc.) show *project-level*
schedules for traders watching sell pressure. Vestream is **personal** — your
wallet, your unlocks, your alerts, your tax report. That's the gap.

---

## Audiences (priority order)

1. **Token recipients** — airdrop farmers, early contributors, team/advisor
   allocations, investors with vesting. Highest intent: they have vesting
   tokens RIGHT NOW and need tracking + tax. This is the core B2C buyer.
2. **Crypto-native retail / traders** who watch unlocks as a sell-pressure
   signal. Lower intent to pay, but huge amplifiers — they share unlock content.
3. **Projects / DAOs distributing tokens** — they can point their vesting
   recipients at Vestream ("track your allocation here"). Partnership channel,
   1 project → hundreds of warm users.
4. **Developers / AI-agent builders** (REST API + MCP) — separate, lower
   priority for an awareness goal. Keep warm, don't lead with it.

## The content engine: "The Unlock Radar"

This is the heartbeat. Repeatable, data-driven, defensible.

| Cadence | Format | Hook |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly (Mon) | "🔓 Unlocking this week" thread — top 5–10 unlocks across all chains, **tag each project** | Becomes a format people follow & projects reshare |
| Daily | "Unlock of the day" — one credible project unlocking, tagged | Cheap, consistent, gets project engagement |
| Weekly (Fri) | "Biggest unlocks NEXT week" preview | Forward-looking → people come back |
| Monthly | "$X unlocking in [Month]" infographic / mini-report | Shareable big-picture asset, PR-able |

**Rules for the engine:**
- Filter OUT pump.fun memecoins and PinkSale microcap dust — only credible
  projects, or it reads as spam and risks the brand.
- Every post CTAs to the free tool or `/unlocks`: *"Curious what's vesting to
  your wallet? → vestream.io"*
- Tag the project's real X handle (verify it — e.g. Orderly's DexScreener TG is
  an impersonation). Tagging gets you in front of THEIR audience for free.

## The viral loop: the free tool as lead magnet

`find-vestings` ("paste your wallet → see what's vesting to you") is the
conversion engine. It's shareable because the result is *personal and
surprising* ("I didn't know I had X vesting").

- Every content piece ends with a CTA to it.
- Pin a post: "Paste your wallet, see every token vesting to you — free."
- The result screen is the natural upsell point to the app (alerts) and Pro
  (tax export).

Funnel: **Content (X/TG) → free wallet scan → app install → Pro.**

## Channels

**A. X/Twitter (primary)**
- The Unlock Radar content engine lives here.
- Reply-guy strategy: jump into unlock/airdrop/tax conversations with genuinely
  useful answers, tool link only when relevant.
- Engage every project you tag — many will reshare.

**B. Telegram / Discord**
- When a project unlocks, its community is actively discussing it. Show up
  **value-first** (answer "when does X unlock / how do I track it") and mention
  Vestream naturally. Never drop-and-run spam — that gets you banned and burns
  the brand.
- Build relationships in a few high-value communities rather than blasting many.

**C. Reddit**
- r/CryptoCurrency, r/ethfinance, r/defi, r/CryptoTax. "How do I track my
  vesting / do taxes on airdrops" is a recurring question — answer it well,
  mention the tool. Evergreen, ranks in Google too.

**D. SEO / owned content (compounding, low-effort-per-unit)**
- You already have `/unlocks`, `/protocols`, `/protocols/[slug]/unlocks`,
  `/resources`. These are **programmatic SEO gold** — every project's unlock
  page can rank for "[project] token unlock schedule."
- Add long-tail articles: "how to track vesting tokens," "crypto vesting tax
  guide," "[protocol] vesting explained."
- This is the channel that keeps working while you sleep — prioritize it.

**E. App Store ASO**
- Optimize listing keywords: "token unlock," "vesting tracker," "crypto unlock
  alerts," "airdrop tax." Title/subtitle + screenshots that show the wallet
  scan result.

**F. Partnerships**
- Vesting protocols (Sablier, Hedgey, etc.): get listed as a tool that supports
  them; co-content.
- Crypto newsletters / mid-tier KOLs in the tax + airdrop niche: complementary,
  not competitive.

## Weekly operating cadence (daily, part-time)

- **Mon:** Publish "Unlocks this week" thread (hero = biggest-recipient unlock).
- **Daily (~30 min):** 1 "unlock of the day" tag post + 20 min replying in
  relevant X/Reddit/TG conversations.
- **Wed:** 1 value/educational post (tax tip, how vesting works, feature
  highlight, a real user's "look what I found vesting" story).
- **Fri:** "Biggest unlocks next week" preview + community engagement.
- **Ongoing:** reply to every project/person who engages; DM 2–3 projects/week
  for partnerships.
- **Monthly:** 1 bigger asset (infographic, monthly unlock report, or blog).

Batch the Unlock Radar posts — pull the data once, schedule the week.

---

## Metrics (review weekly)

| Layer | Metric |
|---|---|
| Awareness | X impressions, profile visits, follower growth |
| Traffic | site visits — especially `/find-vestings` and `/unlocks` |
| Activation | free wallet scans run, app installs |
| Revenue (later) | Pro conversions |

**Rule:** double down on whichever post format drives the most
`/find-vestings` visits. Kill what doesn't. Let data, not taste, allocate effort.

---

## Optional paid layer (when organic CAC is understood)

- Boost the best-performing organic unlock posts (cheapest, lowest-risk start).
- Micro-deals with mid-tier crypto-tax / airdrop-farming accounts.
- App-install campaigns once you know what a free→install→Pro funnel is worth.
Start tiny, scale only what proves ROI.
